Welcome

I am a Jansky fellow at the NRAO in Socorro. I study the earliest phases of high-mass star-formation with my adviser Yancy Shirley. I am also a team member of the Bolocam Galactic Plane Survey (BGPS) collaboration. My research interests center on star-formation, astrochemistry, and radio astronomy.
