Welcome

I am a first year astronomy graduate student at Steward Observatory studying the earliest phases of high mass star formation with my adviser Yancy Shirley. I am also a team member of the Bolocam Galactic Plane Survey (BGPS) collaboration. My research interests center on star formation, radio astronomy, and astrochemistry.
