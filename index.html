Welcome

I am an Assistant Staff Scientist of the National Radio Astronomy Observatory in Socorro, NM. I completed my thesis under the supervision of Prof. Yancy Shirley in 2018, where I was an NSF GRFP Fellow. From 2018 to 2021 I was a Jansky Postdoctoral Fellow of the NRAO. My research focuses on developing innovative techniques to study the dense, molecular interstellar medium of the Milky Way. I use observations from the worlds largest radio telescopes and astrostatistics to understand the earliest evolutionary phases of high-mass star formation. I am also passionate about developing observational methods and open source software tools for radio astronomical applications (see my code page for more details).
