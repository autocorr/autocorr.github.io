Welcome

I am a Jansky Postdoctoral Fellow of the National Radio Astronomy Observatory, Socorro, NM. My research interests include star formation, the interstellar medium, astrochemistry, and radio astronomy techniques. I completed my thesis under the supervision of Prof. Yancy Shirley in 2018 investigating starless clump candidates and the earliest phases of high-mass star formation in the Milky Way. I am a team member in the ALMA Large Programs ALMA-IMF and FAUST.
