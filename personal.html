About Me

Although not known for its numerous sunny days, I grew up in the beautiful Northwest in Puyallup, Washington. My passion for astronomy grew out of my general curiousity about how things work and the encouragement and support I received from my parents. As an avid amateur astronomy, my trust Meade LX10 and I have scanned the skies together since middle school. I received my B.S. in Physics from Western Washington University in 2012 before arriving in sunny Tucson.
